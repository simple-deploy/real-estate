Why Meridian

We Don't Just Sell Properties.
We Protect Your Wealth.

Choosing an agent is one of the most consequential financial decisions you'll make. Here's exactly what makes Meridian different — in plain language.

01

We Average 103% of Asking Price

In 2024, our vendors received an average of 103% of their asking price — while the Sydney market average was 98.4%. That difference on a $3M home is $138,000 more in your pocket. This is the result of strategic pricing, competitive campaigns, and exceptional negotiation.

02

Pre-Qualified Buyer Database of 8,000+

Before your property ever hits realestate.com.au, we've already contacted 8,000+ pre-qualified buyers who've registered specific price ranges, suburb preferences, and property types. Genuine competition drives prices up — and we engineer that competition deliberately.

03

Average 17 Days on Market vs. Sydney's 34

Longer days on market create stigma — buyers wonder why a property is sitting. Our average is 17 days, half the Sydney average, because we enter the market with the right pricing strategy, a prepared property, and an active buyer pool ready to move quickly.

04

Off-Market Access Before Public Listings

Over 30% of our transactions happen before a property is publicly listed. Our private network of buyers and sellers means opportunities that never appear on any portal — for both buyers seeking discretion and vendors wanting a private sale.

05

Boutique Agency — You Deal With Senior Agents Only

We are deliberately small. Every client works directly with a senior agent — not a junior who will hand you off. Victoria and James personally oversee all high-value listings. Boutique service means your property gets the attention it deserves.

06

Fully Managed, Premium Marketing Campaigns

We invest in your property's presentation and marketing because it directly affects your sale price. Professional architectural photography, drone footage, 3D virtual tours, Domain Premier and realestate.com.au premium listings — all included in our service.

07

Transparent Fees — No Hidden Costs

Our commission structure is clearly explained upfront. No surprise marketing levies, no administration fees buried in the fine print. We provide a complete written cost summary before you sign anything. If you don't understand every line item, we haven't done our job.

08

Independent — We Work For You, Not a Franchise

We are proudly independent, not affiliated with any national franchise. Our loyalty is to our clients and our reputation — not a head office quota. This means we can price and negotiate in your best interests at all times, without any conflict of interest.
